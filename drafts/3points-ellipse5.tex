	\section{Intro}
	Let's assume that $x_1, y_1 = (0,0)$ and $x_2 = 0$. Then we have:
	
	\begin{align*}
	E=-Cy_2\\
	Dx_3=-(Ax_3^2 + Bx_3y_3 + C(y_3^2-y_2y_3))\\
	\end{align*}
	
	Renaming things:
	
	\begin{align}
	E=C\beta\\
	D = A\alpha_a + B\alpha_b + C\alpha_c
	\end{align}
